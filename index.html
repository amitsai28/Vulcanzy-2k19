The festive atmosphere mid march was amplified with Vulcancy’18 which spoke out loud with an exciting list of technical events from all the branches namely, Biotechnology, Civil, Chemical, Computer Science, Electrical and Electronics, Electronics and Communication, Mechanical and Metallurgy and materials engineering along with department of Science and Humanities and Innovation club. The Fest had been planned in mid-March under the guidance of Resident coordinator, Prof. S. Srinivasa Rao and fest coordinator, Dr.Ahmed along with the faculty of respective departments. The events were all well coordinated and hard worked by student organisers which in turn were guided by the faculty members. All technical events concluded with great memories and left behind the sense of technical advancements. Technical part of Vulcancy’18 was followd by cultural events on the dates-12thand 13th March. The Technical part of fest ended up pompfully as a result of dedication of all volunteers, organizers, participants and the student technical conveners Mr. Mukul Patel, 3rd year Mechanical and Ms. Sindhu Kotla,3rd year ECE. The fest ended up with a lantern ceremony conducted by Jayanth and team to set the spirits high.
