V U L C A N Z Y - 2 K 1 9

NIT-ANDHRA PRADESH

MARCH 12 - MARCH 15

The fest was diligently organized. There was a soul to it. A soul that was vibrant, contagious and exuberant. At the entrance of the main block were the huge banners denoting the VULCANZY 2K18. The tone of decorations was very festive as you entered the main block of NIT-Andhra Pradesh. Prof. Y.N.Reddy of NIT-Warangal arrived in time along with the guests Prof. Srinivasa Rao of NIT-Andhra Pradesh to the inaugural ceremony and they were received by the fest coordinator Dr. Ahmed.The students welcomed them by presenting blossoming bouquets of flowers and sang the prayer song which is a custom tradition. The inaugural ceremony was filled with all the association and club heads who were enthusiastic and set the mood for the celebrations. Soon they declared the festival open with the lighting of the lamp. Then the respected dignitaries gave their valuable messages, experiences and ideas in the form of speeches to encourage students on taking active participation in the fest.



T E C H N I C A L


The techno-cultural fest, Vulcanzy’18 went leaving behind the memories of teamwork, competitiveness, tech boons and a world full of joy in National Institute of Technology, Andhra Pradesh. The festive atmosphere mid march was amplified with Vulcancy’18 which spoke out loud with an exciting list of technical events from all the branches namely, Biotechnology, Civil, Chemical, Computer Science, Electrical and Electronics, Electronics and Communication, Mechanical and Metallurgy and materials engineering along with department of Science and Humanities and Innovation club. The Fest had been planned in mid-March under the guidance of Resident coordinator, Prof. S. Srinivasa Rao and fest coordinator, Dr.Ahmed along with the faculty of respective departments. The events were all well coordinated and hard worked by student organisers which in turn were guided by the faculty members. All technical events concluded with great memories and left behind the sense of technical advancements. Technical part of Vulcancy’18 was followd by cultural events on the dates-12thand 13th March. The Technical part of fest ended up pompfully as a result of dedication of all volunteers, organizers, participants and the student technical conveners Mr. Mukul Patel, 3rd year Mechanical and Ms. Sindhu Kotla,3rd year ECE. The fest ended up with a lantern ceremony conducted by Jayanth and team to set the spirits high.



C U L T U R A L


Ending the technical events then started the cultural performances hosted on the stage that had been dealt keenly and with lot of efforts set for vulcanzy 2k18. It was a mixture of music, dance and dramas which were performed and showcased perfectly on the stage with having a LED screen as an added advantage. Few highlight performances were • The Mime which showed how Technology is influencing human life and how people are getting addicted to technology and social media. • Chaavat boys: It is a mixture of dance, drama and music focused on entertaining the audience in their own funny ways by various acts and dance steps. It was one of the most loved performance of the night. • Apart from all the amazing stage performances there was an exceptional performance by our students from the Dance Club who respected a farmer’s life .We are so busy with our current life that we tend to forget the little details in our daily life. Everyday tonnes of food is being wasted across the globe. Are we really showing the gratitude towards the food we have everyday? Do we really value the efforts put by a farmer to cultivate? Therefore our students portrayed the difficulties faced by a peasant after a continuous effort everyday, depending upon the climate which always leaves him helpless, and facing heartless money lenders is definitely not a cake walk. • The main attraction that dragged all the students attention was the Ritzy. There was no theme for this round. All the participants dressed up to look up the best and were judged by few faculty based on their walk, attire and few other parameters. The second day got wrapped with a huge number of interesting and entertaining performances. The third day started with promotions of the movie Aithe2.0 which cheered up all the students and it ended up with selfies all over the main block with the cast and crew. With the cultural club taking over the day, a vast number of events like Doodle it, Beat Quest, Whirlophile, Sherlock, Passcode were conducted which showcased the talents of the students. Ending up the events and competitions by the evening, it was the cultural performances that refilled the energy in the students. Few highlights on day3 were • Collaboration between music and drama: This one was the 1st attempt made by two clubs. It was successfully carried out by singers and drama club members performing various songs and act accordingly which audience like a lot. • Ritzy was back again with a bang on the day3 which was on western theme. The legacy of NIT-Andhra Pradesh on closing ceremony of the vulcanzy was a show stopper where students let their lanterns into the dark sky which were dancing a waltz on all souls that night. And then the much awaited DJ night was set to lit the fest. Till the music was gone the students partied on! The students worked hard, played hard and partied even harder. With the DJ night the vulcanzy get’s wrapped up refilling the energy in students amidst the academics and increased the expectations on vulcanzy 2k19.
